**Toilet Warriors** (**[wc]**) (March 2004 - June 2005) was one of the best and most successful clans when talking about competative side of LieroX. A lot of players, specially the older ones who originate from the time of Toilet Warriors era, claim that wc is the best clan to ever exist in the LieroX community and it will not be able to find a follower anytime soon.

Members of the clan were mostly playing Liero v1.0 mod but they were open for other settings as well. Every 'Toileteer', as they would sometimes refer to themselves, was well known for being one of the most active and skilled players of that time.

## History

Clan was formed by real life friends Kot, d0t and Dimon in March of 2004. The idea was to make some catchy and funny sounding name for a clan. Kot or d0t came up with the idea about Toilet Warriors and it was approved by all three guys instantly. The tag was generated later as the word toilet would often ring an association to a water closet. Originally Kot was the leader but he would soon retire from LieroX and leave the first place to Dimon, d0t was named for a co-leader.

Dimon and d0t were the only members for quite some time until they got familiar with [Rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) combo in a clan war versus [Player-Killers](/wiki/index.php/Player-Killers/). After that they started to play only [Rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) and came across KW clan where they met Kalma. Kalma was very interested in the name Toilet Warriors and soon he has joined the clan making Toilet Warriors count three players in clan wars from that point. With Kalma the clan was spreading fast and in no time it would become a group of active and high skilled players. Last to join the clan were Beat and later Drama and Kaptengu, who were originally to play a series of duels in order to declare the one joining the clan. In the end it turned out both were very skilled, active and serious players so they both made it to the clan.

Toilet Warriors retired from active playing in July of 2005. Reason for that was lack of time by most of the members and some of the members leaving their home land to have a further studies abroad. However not all the players left LieroX community. Some moved to another clans but beginning of the Summer in 2005 was a final chapter in the history of Toilet Warriors. Clan's last move was meant to be the Toilet Warriors Fairwell Tournament but it failed due the lack of maturness of contestants involved. Instead they left the community with a farewell movie tribute titled The Legacy Of Toilet Warriors.
