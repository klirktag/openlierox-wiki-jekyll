# [Uw] United Warriors

## History

United warriors was born when two clans, WoF and BH45 decided to ally and merge. BH45 was going to die when almost all active members retired, and WoF didn't have too much active members either. We got an idea to ally from [Osp] MiLeC: "Well you could make pretty nice clan with great potential if you allied with [WoF], both are fast developing clans and together there could be start of great clan.". That's what he said in BH45 thread and the result was United warriors.

## Rules

* No camping, running, cheating etc.
* No multiclanning
* Senior members can start clan wars and recruit trials
* Regular members can participate clan wars
* Trial members can't participate clan wars (Unless seniors wants them to play)

## Recruiting

If you want to be a member:

* You must be good player in many mods
* You must be great guy
* You must be active
* You must be loyal
* All seniors have to accept new members
