**Firebird** is a variation of a limited setting in [Liero v1.0](/wiki/index.php/Liero_1.0/) mod which originates from [Liero Firebird Tournament](/wiki/index.php/Liero_Firebird_Tournament/). Although the setting was originally meant for 20 % loading time it gets often associated with 100 % loading time. Firebird was quite popular in 2004 but later it got replaced by [Limited](/wiki/index.php/Limited/) setting causing only few members of the community remembering the setting and even fewer actually playing it today.

## Weapon list

* Bazooka
* Cannon
* Chaingun
* Dart
* Doomsday
* Handgun
* Flamer
* Grasshopper
* Shotgun
