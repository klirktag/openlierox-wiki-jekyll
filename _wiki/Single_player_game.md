**Single player** support was added in [OpenLieroX](/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/). It is intended mostly to provide tutorial levels for newcomers but it may also be used for more complex stuff. This probably will be extended a bit later on.

Single player games aren't really something new. They are just a list of scripted [Gusanos Maps](/wiki/index.php/Gusanos_Maps/). Or you can also just setup any custom game for one game level.

[Read here for some more information.](/forum/index.php/topic,12829.0.html)
