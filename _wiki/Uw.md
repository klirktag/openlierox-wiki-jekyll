**United warriors** (**[Uw]**)

## History

United warriors born when two clans, WoF and BH45 decided to ally and merge. BH45 was going to die when almost all active members retired, and WoF didn't have too much active members either. We got an idea to ally from [Osp] MiLeC: "Well you could make pretty nice clan with great potential if you allied with [WoF], both are fast developing clans and together there could be start of great clan.". That's what he said in BH45 thread and result was United warriors.
