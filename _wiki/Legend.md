Legend is a veteran LX player, whose overwhelming strenght tends to terrify players. He is famed for his ability to success in everything he sets out to do, nothing stops him for accomplishing his goals. Usually he targets the good for larger group, but sometimes he can do reckless moves in his own ends.

He has played more or less major role in each three annuit Coeptis eras, and also being part of United warriors and later in a significant position in World of vanishing Warriors, the clan that continued Uw's legacy. Currently he repserents annuit Coeptis once again, and gladly destroys anyone who is dumb enough to challenge him.
