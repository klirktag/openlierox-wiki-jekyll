<p>Based on <a href="/wiki/index.php/Liero/">Liero</a> gameplay, <a href="/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/">OpenLieroX</a> is an extremely addictive realtime worms shoot-em-up backed by an active gamers community. The game is programmed by Dark Charlie, <a href="/wiki/index.php/User_Albert/">Albert</a>, and Pelya.</p>
<p>If you are new to the game, check out <a href="/wiki/index.php/Liero_1.0/">Liero_1.0</a>. This mod is the most played mod, and the article has all the information needed to jump into the community. You can also find popular mods played on the bottom of that article.</p>
<p>If you are looking for info on customization you can check here. Each article has a guide to it, so you can find all the info you about this here.</p>
