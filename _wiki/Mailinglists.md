Via mailinglists, you can easily make longer discussions in some group via mails and every other member of the mailinglist will receive all posted mails.

# OpenLieroX development mailinglist

[OpenLieroX](/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/) has currently one active mailinglist for development discussions, *openlierox-devel*.

* [Register here to openlierox-devel](https://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/openlierox-devel)
* [Archive of all mails of openlierox-devel](http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?forum_name=openlierox-devel)
