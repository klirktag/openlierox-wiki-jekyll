The ingame console allows the execution of any ingame command. The the [list of commands](/wiki/index.php/Commands/) for further information about the commands.

The ingame console also comes with autocompletion support. Just press TAB-key to get either a list of all available completions or to get the next completion if it is unique. This also works for the parameter of some commands. For example, to get a list of all interesting ingame variables, you may do this:

```
setVar GameOptions.GameInfo.[TAB]
```
