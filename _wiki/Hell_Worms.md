Hell Worms was a successful lt 100% playing clan that existed from October 2004 to January 2006.

## History

![](/wiki/images/thumb/a/a4/Wcvshw.png/340px-Wcvshw.png)

The clan was formed in October 2004 by Holzwurm. Quite soon Lusmu, AGENT, Kurt and some others joined. It quickly rose from a newbie clan to one of the best v1.0 clans. The clan fell apart and inactivity struck after co-leader Kurt left the clan to join [Oldschool Players](/wiki/index.php/Oldschool_Players/) in January 2006.
