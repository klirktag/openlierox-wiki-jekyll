## Unlimited

This setting uses more weapons, and would normally be played with either [Liero 1.0](/wiki/index.php/Liero_1.0/) or [Liero 1.11](/wiki/index.php/Liero_1.11/). Liero 1.11 is usually chosen because of the anti-lag features of it. This setting is played with 100% [Loading Time](/wiki/index.php/Loading_Time/).

### Weapon List

* Bazooka
* Big Nuke
* Blaster
* Cannon
* Chaingun
* Chiquita Bomb
* Cluster Bomb
* Dart
* Doomsday
* Explosives
* Flamer
* Guass Gun
* Handgun
* Larpa
* Laser
* Mini Nuke
* Mini Rockets
* Napalm
* Shotgun
* Super Shotgun
* Uzi
* Winchester
