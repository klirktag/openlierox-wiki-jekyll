**Let's Cheat Death** (**[LcD]**) (March 2005 - August 2006) was a mostly North American clan which was brought up as a merger between [Nigga Blasta-Assault Team](/wiki/index.php/Nigga_Blasta-Assault_Team/) and Advanced Liero Tactics clans.

## History

Let's Cheat Death was formed on March 19, 2005 by Avye, Cloud, and Gigolo as a result of a merger between NB-AT and ALT clans.

On April 20, 2005 Cloud left the 'Death Cheaters' and LcD clan nearly took a spill, but thanks to Gigolo's efforts the clan was kept together.

History of the clan after that is a bit unclear because a lot of members, including some old ones, were joining and leaving the clan or they went inactive. However clan was overall alive and pretty active until it finally died in August of 2006.
