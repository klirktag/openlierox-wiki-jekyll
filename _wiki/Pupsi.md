## Pupsi's Skin Competition

Pupsi has hosted 2 Skin Competitions. The idea was for every contestant to create a Skin. After the deadline people would choose and list first 3 Skins (1-3 points). Skin with most points has won in the end.

* [Pupsi's Skin Competition GU thread](http://www.thegaminguniverse.com/forums/showthread.php?t=14776)
* [Pupsi's Skin Competition 2 GU thread](http://www.thegaminguniverse.com/forums/showthread.php?t=15634)
