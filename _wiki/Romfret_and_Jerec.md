### [RaJ] Romfret and Jerec

This is a retired clan. There were 4 founders who all came from France. Leaders were [Romfret](/wiki/index.php/Romfret/) and [Jerec](/wiki/index.php/Jerec/). Co-leaders, who had around the sam rights as them, were [Calvi](/wiki/index.php/Calvi/) and Max.
From the leaders, only [Calvi](/wiki/index.php/Calvi/) is still active. From the members, there are still active players like [Raziel](/wiki/index.php/Raziel/) for exemple.
