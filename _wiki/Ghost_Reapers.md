**Ghost Reapers** (**[GR]**) ( May 2004 - July 2004) was a [Lamda Bunker](/wiki/index.php/Lamda_Bunker/) [Rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) playing clan. It was being recognized for the rule which stated that every member must use the Ghost skin. On 22nd of July in 2004 Ghost Reapers renamed themselves to [United States of America](/wiki/index.php/United_States_of_America/) clan and started recruiting only North American players.
