**Ancient Warriors** (**(AW)**) (November 2004 - December 2004) was a strictly 100 % loading time playing clan made by Miniman. It has later merged with [Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Millenium_Warriors/) to form [Ancient Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Ancient_Millenium_Warriors/).

## History

Miniman left BtBs because of clan's inactivity and together with ReddPuke they formed Ancient Warriors. In late December 2004 clan has merged with [Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Millenium_Warriors/) into [Ancient Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Ancient_Millenium_Warriors/).
