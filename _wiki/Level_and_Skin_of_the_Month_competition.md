**Level and Skin of the Month** (**LoM/SoM**) (September, October 2004 and January, February, March 2005, 2008+) was a competition being hosted by [Jukke](/wiki/index.php/Jukke/) and [HocusPocus](/wiki/index.php/HocusPocus/). The goal was for community to select the best Level and Skin of the month so it could be put up on the official LieroX home page. Competition is active again in 2008.

## History

Contest originates from Level of the Month competition which was first hosted by [Jukke](/wiki/index.php/Jukke/) in September 2004. After a successful conclusion the contest got expanded to Level and Skin of the Month (shortened to "LoM & SoM", later "LoM/SoM") competition in October 2004.

```
The first ever Level of the Month voting has ended. Overall there was a total of 20
votes sent which is fairly good for the first time I think. More votes for the next LoM
would be nice. The anticipation is at its peak. Who will emerge victorious and rise to
fame and glory? After counting, double checking and slightly even triple checking the
votes the official results have been made. Congratulations to all voted levels and
their makers! ~ Jukke, closing the first competition
```

In December 2004 Jukke was absent from the LieroX cumminity for a longer period. He was searching for a host to replace him for the time of his absence. Although several voulenteers raised their hands the competition was never started for December. In January Jukke has returned and together with HocusPocus they hosted LoM/SoM for January 2005 in March 2005. Last competition was hosted in April for March 2005.

Competition was revived in 2008 with a slight change of the system. The period was narrowed down to 1/3 of a year and Mod section was added to the voting. Basic principles stayed same.

## Rules

Participants would pick in their opinion the best 3 (5 today) Levels and Skins (and Mods today) from the nominee list by ranking them from first to third place. Each user could vote only once and if he wished so, he was able to only cast the first place vote, first and second place vote, first and second place vote only in one category etc. If a participant was also author of the Level or Skin nominated, he was forbidden to vote for his own creation. When nominees have been posted a deadline was set. The day after the voting has ended host would count the votes and announce the results. Top three Levels and Skins were then posted in the LieroX official home page.

## Ranking system

* A Level/Skin voted #1 in a vote received 3 points.
* A Level/Skin voted #2 in a vote received 2 points.
* A Level/Skin voted #3 in a vote received 1 point.

The points were in the end counted together for each Level and Skin voted for and the scores were published after the voting time has ended.

In 2008 system was modified a bit. 5 points are awarded to the Level/Mod/Skin voted first as there are 5 places for stuff to be voted on.
