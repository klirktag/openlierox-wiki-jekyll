## Loading Time

Loading Time is the amount of time it takes for a weapon to reload its clips. It ranges from 0 to 500. This speed also changes with different mods. This is because when you make weapons you have a choice on how long you want weapons to reload for. Using an amount to fill the clip. The smaller the number, the longer it will take to reload.

You can change the loading time by going into Game Settings/Options in your server and adjusting the bar. If you move it left it will get lower and faster, moving it right will have the opposite and increase the speed.
