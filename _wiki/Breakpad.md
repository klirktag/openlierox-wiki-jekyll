[Google Breakpad](http://code.google.com/p/google-breakpad/) is a library which automatically gets human readable information out of a crash. This is used in [OpenLieroX](/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/) in case of a crash to get all important information and to prepare it all together for an automatic crash report.
