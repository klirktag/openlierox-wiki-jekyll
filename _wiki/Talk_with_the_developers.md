Talking with the [developers](/wiki/index.php/Developers/) is the best way to get involved with the [OpenLieroX](/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/) project. There are multiple ways you can contact us. Best way is to leave a mail in our [mailinglist](/wiki/index.php/Mailinglist/), i.e. at [openlierox-devel@lists.sourceforge.net](mailto:openlierox-devel@lists.sourceforge.net).
