**Gaming Universe**

## History

JasonB asked to have LieroX put on the boards of The Gaming Universe to get people interested in his project. He gathered quite a few people to help him build up his game. Eventually the forums became to hold such boards such as Clan Threads and Downloadable User Content, help and support forums and became the home of LieroX players.
