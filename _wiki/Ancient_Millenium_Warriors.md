**Ancient Millenium Warriors** (**(AMW)**) (December 2004 - July 2005) was a strictly 100 % loading time playing clan made as a merger of [Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Millenium_Warriors/) and [Ancient Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Ancient_Warriors/).

## History

Clan was made by -Liquid-, Andy Tran and Miniman as a merger between [Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Millenium_Warriors/) and [Ancient Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Ancient_Warriors/). After a certain period clan got very inactive, most likely the only active player was Loop who left AMW to join HW in end of June 2005.
