## [pC] pugnaCitas (The Desire to Fight)

[pC] was a legendary LX clan which dominated the clan war scene during it's existence. The clan was thought to be a successor of [Toilet Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Toilet_Warriors/). Though it consisted of many members, the great success of the clan was provided by the Kemp and QuafaS duo.

## History

* 17th of June 2005 - the clan was created former [wc] Kemp, [Osp] QuafaS and [WaN] Morbid. On the same day Gyogyi joined.
* 27th of June 2005 - Funkkarimies is granted [pC] trialship.
* 28th of June 2005 - Drama, using the nick *Dominator*, is granted [pC] trialship.
* 18th of July 2005 - Drama and Funkkarimies become full members of [pC]
* 20th of September 2005 - Former [wc] Rex joins [pC]
* 7th of January 2006 - Turma and Chase join [pC]
* 5th of February 2006 - [pC] loses their first cw against [Let's Cheat Death](/wiki/index.php/Let's_Cheat_Death/) and Gyogyi leaves the clan.
* 18th of February 2006 - Inactive members are kicked.
* February 2006 - [pC] loses their second ever cw against Experienced worms
* 28th of March 2006 - Chase and Turma leave [pC] to create Half-Lifers
* 16th of April 2006 - Drama returns to LX
* 22nd of July 2006 - [pC] clan retires from professinal LX gaming

## Controversy

The reasons of [pC] retiring remain unsure. Inactivity for one was definitely a part of it. QuafaS and Kemp getting fed up with LX might have mattered too. Some speculate that argues between [Osp] and [pC] caused the fall of [pC]. This might be somewhat true, because [pC]'s certain actions in clan wars caused some annoyance among [Osp] members. This is also where [Osp] MileC gained his nickname *MileC the clan destroyer*. Due to his argues with pC members Kemp declared that he's sick of LX.
