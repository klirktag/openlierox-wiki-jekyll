[MaW] - Masters @ Work

## History

* [MaW] was born on the 7th of April 2008 after Pandora and Kurt both returned to LX after a near 6-month break. Since forming the clan has dominated LX cw scene and holds first place in [Top Clans League](/wiki/index.php/Top_Clans_League/). Kurt left the clan on the 18th of May 2008.

* [MaW] pretty much plays all the settings and levels available in their little yellow LX folder.
* For contacting, ask for MSN contacts from clan thread, via PM or catch us in game.
* Trial members aren't allowed to play in clan wars, Masters (Regular members) aren't allowed to start a clan war without the permission from the Grandmaster (Leaders).
* Joining this little clan is ultimately hard and painful process, there's no point of trying unless you really really want to, else it's just a waste of time.
