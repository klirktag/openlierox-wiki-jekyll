**Liero Xtreme v0.56 Christmas Edition** (or **LieroX-Mas**, **LieroX-Mastreme**) is a Liero Xtreme v0.56 pack featuring only Christmas related files. It was mostly done by [SimpleX Modding team](/wiki/index.php/SimpleX_Modding_team/) and their LieroX-Mas Project but it also includes Christmas related skins which were existing before the pack was released on December 17th, 2004.

## Pack includes

### Mods

* LieroX-Mas Mod v1.1

### Levels

* ChristmasTree
* DownTown3-Organisation
* Gingerbread
* GingerbreadMirrored
* North Star
* santaworkshop
* Snow Level
* SnowFlakes2

### Skins

* Bikini
* Elf
* GingerbreadMan
* HankeyA
* HankeyB
* Present Sack
* Snowman
* X-MasWorm
* X-MasWorm_Cane
* YoshiRudolf
* YoshiSanta

### Other files

* LieroX-Mas Frontend
