**doh** (**[doh]**) (February 2004 - September 2004) was one of the early LieroX clans, created by [MasterOfHyrule](/wiki/index.php/MasterOfHyrule/).

## History

### 1st period

Clan was created by MOH in February 2004. He recruited several members and stopped when clan would count 8 members. 1st period of doh clan has ended in late March 2004 with MOH leaving to join [erm](/wiki/index.php/Erm/) clan. Before he left he would choose a new leader to lead the doh clan into the 2nd period.

### 2nd period

Raven has been chosen for a new leader. Clan was successfully going on without MOH until it got a bit inactive in May 2004 when Raven had a computer problem. About at the same time one of the original members - Bu||et has left the clan to create his own clan. Despite the troubles facing clan held on.

### 3rd period

In August 2004 Andy Tran took the lead when Raven got inactive and has left the clan because of that. There was hardly any doh member seen causing Andy Tran joining BtBs in September 2004 and finishing the period of doh clan.
