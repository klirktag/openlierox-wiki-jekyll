**[CB] - Clan Butchery**

## Basic info(written by Hurme)

Well, firstly this clan was founded in Fall 2003, although we weren't very active back then. When we started playing LieroX, we decided to found our own clan, because there were five of us in the very beginning. That also secured our survival and so we are still live and kickin' after two years. (Well, not anymore, but we were at the time I wrote this. :P)

The founders all live within a 200m radius from each other, and all of them have played the original Liero pretty actively for at least 3 years, except the leaders, who have played Liero VERY actively for 3-4 years. And by "VERY actively", I also mean it. Almost everyday, that is :P. Our strong experience with the original Liero was partially responsible for the idea of our own clan at the first place, and now I can safely say that "Hey, it wasn't so bad idea after all" :P. Our Golden Rule became a standard to all members when we started playing actively in November 2004. That rule has gained us a nice reputation among the LieroX community. (At least among those whose have even heard of us.)

The other members have joined [CB] in 2005, and they have showed to be one of those rare exceptions, who can respect our Golden Rule. (Hey, players like them are NOT easy to find :-\ , we had to play for a pretty long time to even find one new member.) And of course they can play atleast as good as the other members.
