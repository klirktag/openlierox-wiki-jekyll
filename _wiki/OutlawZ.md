**OutlawZ** (**[OwZ]**)

## Clan description

### Goal

OwZ is a clan where everyone is equal. There are no leaders. There are no ranks. All the members are equally important to the clan, and all of them have an equal saying in clan decisions. It's basically what a clan should be like with no beurocracy, just members. This way everyone feel like they actually can do something and mean something for the clan apart from just playing cw's. This way we also can't have too many members in the clan, since it would become impossible to make decisions there. This way the clan wont radically change from day one to day two, since all the members in the clan have agreed on the structure and what it should be like, there are no need for changes, there are no need for new members, albeit we will of course be interested to have like-minded individuals joining us. We are not tryhing to raise an empire like some clans do. We are not trying to become omnipotent in LX. What we are trying is to, as time passes, be known as the best, made by the best, and remain the best for as long as possible. We have ambition and we have talent, we have an eye for talent and we have confidence. Withouth them you will get nowhere. So watch out for us, all of those things you, i and every lx player hige for, may very well become true for us. And once we have it, we sure as hell wont let it slip between our hands!
