**Millenium Warriors** (**(MW)**) (October 2004 - December 2004) was a clan formed by Andy Tran and -Liquid- when BtBs got too inactive. Clan has later merged with [Ancient Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Ancient_Warriors/) into [Ancient Millenium Warriors](/wiki/index.php/Ancient_Millenium_Warriors/).

## History

After [doh](/wiki/index.php/Doh/), history has repeated with [BulleT The Blue Sky](/wiki/index.php/BulleT_The_Blue_Sky/) for Andy Tran. Together with Liquid they have left BtBs and formed Millenium Warriors.
