There are several different **game modes** in [OpenLieroX](/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/).

* Deathmatch
* Team Deathmatch
* Tag
* Demolition
* Capture The Flag (CTF)
* Hide & Seek
* Race
* Team Race

It is not really that hard to develop your own game mode. You can read about this [here](/forum/index.php/topic,12112.0.html).
