# Ninja rope

At any time you may shoot out a ninja rope that will start reeling you in once it hits an obstacle (NB: not when hitting a worm). If you miss the target with the rope, it will fall to the ground and stay there. You can release the rope by firing it again or pressing the jump button.

Classic/modern.
