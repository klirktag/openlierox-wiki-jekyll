**BulleT The Blue Sky** (**(BtBs)**) (June 2004 - November 2004) was a 100 % loading time playing clan formed by Bu||et.

## History

Clan was made in June 2004 by Bu||et as a result of [doh](/wiki/index.php/Doh/)'s inactivity.

In October 2004 a lot of members has left (see Early left BtBs under Members) which caused the final end of BtBs in November 2004 when Bu||et has joined meh clan.

In September 2005 Bu||et tried to revive BulleT The Blue Sky but it took him less than a month to realise this will not work.

## BtBs Level Contest

BtBs has hosted 2 Level Contests. The idea was simple, contestants had to create a new level which was then being judged by several referees. Artist with the most points has won.
