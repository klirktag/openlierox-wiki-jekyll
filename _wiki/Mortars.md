## Mortars

Mortars is a [Combo](/wiki/index.php/Combo/) game in [Modern Warfare](/wiki/index.php/Modern_Warfare/) [Mod](/wiki/index.php/Mod/) with all the weapons beside Mortar Launcher banned or forbidden to pick up. It is most often played with 20% [Loading Time](/wiki/index.php/Loading_Time/).
