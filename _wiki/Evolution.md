Evolution ([e3])

## History

A clan formed by [RuNyoufool](/wiki/index.php/RuNyoufool/), Fierce and [AnubisBlessing](/wiki/index.php/User_AnubisBlessing/) in late March 2006. The clan got it's initial members from merging parts of three clans; waA , [nG](/wiki/index.php/New_Generation/) and [WaN](/wiki/index.php/We_are_Noobs/).
The clan died around summer 2007. Most of their members joined [RIP](/wiki/index.php/Rot_In_Pieces/)
