## General information

**Aegir**, referred as **Krystal** in the [Liero Xtreme Alliance](/forum/), is a [Swedish](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sweden) Liero Xtreme player who has been playing the game since late Summer 2006. He has been in several clans, including [VIP](/wiki/index.php/Viking_Invincible_Pantheon/), [aC](/wiki/index.php/Annuit_Coeptis/), [(V)](/wiki/index.php/Men_of_Valor/), [T3](/forum/index.php/topic,1732.0.html), etc.
He is currently taking part of the clan [Like a Boss](/wiki/index.php/Like_a_Boss/).

## History

Aegir started to play with various nicknames, like **Mr. White**, **Ninja Guardian** and other cool-looking nicknames, in 2006. He enjoyed to play a lot of [mortars](/wiki/index.php/Mortars/) because that was the only thing he could access. He learned fairly quickly to use [combo](/wiki/index.php/Combo/).
At the same time, his brother who was in the clan T$P, bothered him a lot. Because there was only one computer available, his brother played a lot more often. However, his brother met up with [OP](/wiki/index.php/Lodur/) and they became good friends. When T$P fell apart, OP left to join the clan sfm and it was time for Aegir to make an appearance. He kept playing mortars and later on befriended Mashkito, the leader of sfm, and also OP. He joined |sfm| as **Cruncher** slightly before OP left to join Finno-12. It was by that time that Aegir wanted to compete with OP to become the better player. He started to play even more mortars. It was around then when he met one of his loyal friends **Evilninja**, later known as **Svipdag** and Quaqa. They started to play much together.

In the late Fall the same year, he discovered another playable mod in LX - [Liero v.10 100% loading time](/wiki/index.php/Liero_1.0/). He thought that it was awesome, so he started to search for such servers.
There was a server he was very fund of, which was hosted by [Screams](/wiki/index.php/RIP/) from the clan RIP. Screams let him play in his server as much as he liked, despites that the more comfortable players didn't like it. One time, he was alone with many players from RIP.
Screams advertised him about Liero Xtreme Alliance, which was a kind of a new community. Chruncher didn't really appreciate it though, but kept playing Liero v1.0.

In the early 2007, Loke, Achilles and [Lodur](/wiki/index.php/Lodur/) invited him to join VIP - Viking Invincible Pantheon. It was the first clan that had invited him, and it was a clan that played Liero v1.0 as well. He joined the clan, but for one circumstance - That he had to change his Liero Xtreme nickname into a Norse God. That was then the name Aegir was given to the player.
