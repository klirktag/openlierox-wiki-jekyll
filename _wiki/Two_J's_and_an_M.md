## History

At a night in a little village in the Netherlands, there were three boys who were drinkin' some beer, smokin' some spliffs, and talkin' about nonsense.
These three boys were called Jordy (Screams), Jan remmelt (Sneaky) and Mart (Beat).
While they continued talkin' nonsense the subject liero became pretty populair. After talkin' about all players in lx that anoyed them or wich they like they thought like:"we should make like a little clan with the three of us, wich we play with rarely and no one knows its us". (later on I told my fellow toiletteers but lets keep with the subject) Well in our by that time pretty drunk state we thought up the name 2JM, wich was comming from our three names. Then we started thinkin' about names... "WOA! We should be like a godlike clan!" shouted someone, dunno who it was. And that is how we all thought up our own name. After playing with those nick rarely like half a year [wc] died. That is the point I decided I would carry on with my 2JM nick, and so did sneaky and screams. And that my friends.. is how 2JM came to what it is now :)
