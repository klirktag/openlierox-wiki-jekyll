**Angels of Death** (**{AOD}**) (June/July 2004 - 9th of August 2006)

## History

When [Asrack](/wiki/index.php/User_Asrack/) changed his name to hide away from some people, him and [Sirus](/wiki/index.php/Sirus/) made a new clan to play what ever they wanted. They played 40% loading time with just a bunch of friends. Soon to come to the clan and change it was [Magus](/wiki/index.php/Magus/). Magus made the clan a [rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) and [limited](/wiki/index.php/Limited/) playing clan. AOD clan still played their beloved 40 lt, but it was slowly changing. Eventually they took the clan to [Gaming Universe](/wiki/index.php/Gaming_Universe/) and after a few clan wars, Asrack and [Magus](/wiki/index.php/Magus/) joined [Let's Cheat Death](/wiki/index.php/Let's_Cheat_Death/), which meant the end of Angels of Death.
