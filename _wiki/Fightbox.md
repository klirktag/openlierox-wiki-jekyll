# FightBox tournament

Fightbox (FB) is the largest and one of the most successful gaming competitions in Liero Xtreme history. It spans hundreds of rounds with dozens of registered players. The goal of the competition is to provide fun and action-packed games in a multitude of settings. Each round is a competition in itself, and over time, victories from the rounds add up to long-time scores and awards. Fightbox is being actively played by peoples who plays much LX and cares about joy of the community. Fightbox will use any Open LieroX which is an open source project from Lieroextreme wich is being actively developed by Dark Charlie and [RIP](/wiki/index.php/RIP/) clan.

The creator of the competition is MiLeC. Fightbox is LXA hosted tourny. There are several competition hosts, who are taking care of round hosting. Main competition host is Cloud. LegendKiller, Desmond and Kunoichi are functioning as co-competition hosts. A Fightbox round starts when one of the competition hosts decides to host a server called Fightbox. Any registered player may join the competition at that time. Before the competition starts, registered players online in MSN will be informed that Fightbox is being hosted.

## History

Fightbox got its start when I was getting bored to camping and lack of competitions. I decided to start my own fight against major camping and I started to find out rules wich would make gaming more intensive.
Then I figured out this competition to be something that there havent ever been. Not so serious but very active competition where you can smell that it's competition in the air! Whole Fightbox 2005 was developing of the rules to be ideal for intensive gaming. Also that's why there was rule "host can change rules" and it still does exist for the same reason, that I can develope my project.
I got current form of rules ready under the FB 2005. It started to grow with speed during FB 2005 and it became one of the most popular competitions.
Because Osp has affected to me that I had started to play multitude mods I decided FB to support mods. I have good example of this mod supporting. When FB 2005 was on it's midpoint 50% of players in it didn't have 8-bit warfare 1.2 and stubbornly I kept playing it and asked players to take it if intrested. Nowdays every FB player has it and it has became generally very popular mod, so FB got it's place in LX community. FB can be used to advertaise maps and mods because it has became popular and it is fun way to do it.

Then I got idea that FB should offer better possibilityes for N/A players and I contacted Cloud who was intrested to be Co-Host he has hosted rounds at N/A primetime and it has became popular also in N/A.
Fierce also became Co-Host, but so far he hasn't hosted at all. He has made the logo of the FB, and I am looking forward to co-operate with him more in future, and mayby he starts host more in future.

I also would like that N/A and European players would both got much cahnces to get win's, but the fact stays that LX is more popular in europe so it will always be European based competition and it would be end of it if only N/A host.
Thats the general history of FB.

### Rules

* There is a total of 500 rounds.

* Each round victory is worth one point for the player, one point for the clan, and one point for the nation. The points are always counted for the player's clan from the beginning, if player will change clan during tourny, his stats won't be updated anymore and he can't represent new clan. Clan leaver can only take part to basic competition. The points are valid until the end of the tournament. It is not possible to remove scores.

* Each round is a deathmatch. Lives are set to 3, maximum kills to 7, and bonuses off. The player that obtains 7 kills wins the round. If no player reaches 7 kills, the player with the most kills wins. If several players are in a tie for most kills, the player who stayed alive the longest wins. If round takes too long, host can put clock on and players have one minute to finish round. If the time runs out, the round is re-arranged.

* There must be atleast 6 players to start a Fightbox round.

* Committing suicide on purpouse during a round is not allowed. Host will consider this issues, case by case and doubtful suicides will result warning and possaible kick from the server. If this happens again it may result even a ban from contest.

* Typekilling and killing a player that is away is allowed.

* It is not allowed to constantly run away, camp, whine or flame. Such actions will result in a warning or in the player being kicked.

* A player having too much lag during the games results in the player being kicked. Do not download during the games. Distance lag is not a problem

* Host wins are not part of the competition. The host tries to offer a challenge to the players and to keep the standard for winning high.

* Good gamesmanship, fair play, and a good spirit are a part of the competition. There is no need to take the competition too seriously. Rather come and enjoy fun games with experienced players!

* There will be played also special rounds, rules in those changes very much but commonly those are just larger rounds, rules are told always before round on the server.

* Torunament rules might change during the tourny, if it makes tourny more succesful.

* New rule: Playing unsportsmanlike can cause you to be suspended for so many games. So if a hosts thinks you are running, you might be suspended for the next 50 games.

* New rule: Suiciding, whether purpose or accident results in 1 less kills.

* New rule: First to 7 kills win(and 10 in special rounds) However, if the max limit is not reached and there is a tie between two people, whoever got the highest kills the fastest wins.

### Settings

Fightbox is a competition which tests the skills of a player in a multitude of settings, and thereby promotes the playing of various mods and levels. Mods and levels with great potential are added to the Fightbox list. The Fightbox hosts are always ready to listen to suggestions for new possible settings. The following lists contains the settings currently played in Fightbox:

* 1. [Liero v1.0, 100% lt, non-combo, limited weapons](/wiki/index.php/Limited/) (banned weapons include unbalanced types and spammy ones)
* 2. [Liero v1.0, 20% lt, combo](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) (Rifle, Gauss gun, and Napalm)
* 3. [MSF II](/wiki/index.php/MSF_II/) 100% lt, non-combo
* 4. Liero v1.11, 100% lt, non-combo, all weapons (Zimm and all Mines are however banned)
* 5. Modern Warfare v1.0, 100% lt, non-combo, limited weapons
* 6. Foodfight v0.6, 100% lt, limited, non-combo
* 7. 8-bit Warfare, 100% lt, limited, non-combo
* 8. WH40K, 100% lt, limited, non-combo
* 9. Shock v1.40 air, 100%lt, limited, non-combo

### What you need to succeed

Winning a round in Fightbox requires a substantial amount of skills in the settings that are currently played. You'll need to play aggressvely to obtain the kills needed to win the round. And ofcourse you'll have to behave decently to even get to play. The best way learn is simply to play and enjoy the games! Becoming one of the top players such as King of the Fightbox requires alot of participation, and shows that you are a multi-dimensional player with high level skills in a wide array of settings.

Awards

* King of the Fightbox: Leader of the tourny after 500 round
* King of the first quater: Best player during the rounds 1-125
* King of the second quater: Best player during the rounds 125-250
* King of the third quater: Best player during the rounds 250-375
* King of the fourth quater: Best player during the rounds 375-500
* 2nd to 10th place players gets awarded.
* Master Clan of the Fightbox: Top clan after 500 rounds
* Best Nation of the Fightbox: Top nation after 500 rounds
* Longest Winning Streak Award: Longest winning streak made during the tourny
* Agressivity award : Most kills/rounds player in all quaters and after 500 rounds
* Massmurderer award: Most kills player in all quaters and after 500 rounds
* Ironman award: Best Kill/deaths ratio player in all quaters and after 500 rounds
* Award for every Nation's Top Soldier: After 500 rounds best player of every country gets awarded
* Awards for specialround winners
* Friendliest player in Fightbox: Voted by participants in FB
* They love my style award: Voted after the tourny, criteria is most entertaining playingstyle.
