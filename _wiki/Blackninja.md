**Blackninja** (also known as **BNX**) (active in late 2003 - early 2007) was a leader of [erm](/wiki/index.php/Erm/) clan and later a co-leader in [42](/wiki/index.php/42/) clan, mod of LieroX section in [Gaming Universe](/wiki/index.php/Gaming_Universe/), channel OP in LieroX IRC channel, owner of [LieroX.net](/wiki/index.php/LieroX.net/) portal and a contributor of LieroX files.
