## (V) - Men of Valor

### History

After Asrack and Spectro left [Let's Cheat Death](/wiki/index.php/Let's_Cheat_Death/) - [LcD], Asrack went back to his old clan [Angels of Death](/wiki/index.php/Angels_of_Death/) - {AOD}, and Spectro went solo for awhile. Asrack kept asking Spectro to join {AOD}, but he didn't like the tag. So Asrack suggested they changed the tag, and just start over. Spectro agreed. After 2 hours of planning they merged with [GC] and invited Tizzle to be co-leader as well and after another 6 hours of planning (V) - Men of Valor was formed.

Men of Valor was liked for it's funny attitude but strong gaming, the clan was around for 2 years before it was finally laid to rest by the last remaining leader Spectro. It was given a good bye in 2 pages and was closed and archived on LieroX alliance.
