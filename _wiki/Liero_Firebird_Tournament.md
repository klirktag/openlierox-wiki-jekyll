**Liero Firebird Tournament** (often just **Firebird** or **Firebird Tournament**) was a deathmatch tournament being hosted by Fierce in 2004 and 2005. 3 rounds of the tournament were successfully played. This is one of the first tournaments of this kind (possibly the first one) in LieroX.

## Rules

Tournament consisted of 16 players being divided into 4 elimination groups. 2 players from each group - winner of the match and the one with most kills (excluding the winner) - would advance into the playoffs making the total of 8 players in the semi finals. First part of the playoffs was being divided into 2 groups with 4 players where only 2 players would make it into the finals. Best 4 players were in the end competing for the final victory. All the fights were being supervisored and hosted by Fierce, his achievement in those matches did not count. If a player timed out durring the match, the game went on without him. One of the most important rules included player picking only one of each weapons.

## Controversy

Through the tournament some of the lag issues were present as there have been problems with internet providers. In the second tournament some late announcements were made which caused final game consisting only of TsL clan members. For the third tournament there has been a discussion brought up to change the 20 % loading time to 100 % loading time but host of the tournament wanted to remain the original setting.
