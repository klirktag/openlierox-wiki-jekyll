**Liero 1.0** is one of the most popular mods in Liero Xtreme. It is also the default mod and the most respectfully played [mod](/wiki/index.php/Mod/) in the game.

## Settings

The two most popular settings in this mod is [Rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) and [Limited](/wiki/index.php/Limited/). Played well by almost any respectable clan. Some other settings that are not played as much are [Firebird](/wiki/index.php/Firebird/) and [Unlimited](/wiki/index.php/Unlimited/). A list can be found below.

* [Only rifles at 20% loading time](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/)
* [Limited weapons at 100% loading time](/wiki/index.php/Limited/)
* [Firebird](/wiki/index.php/Firebird/)
* [A cast of weapons played at 100% loading time](/wiki/index.php/Unlimited/)
