A level set in a bunker which is almost exclusively played in [rifle games](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/). Made by Ragzouken.

## Layout

The straight narrow passages provide a unique style of play. The top and bottom part of the level, displayed as rock is all dirt. In the far east and west parts of the level it can be dug out to reveal paths into the main area of the level.
