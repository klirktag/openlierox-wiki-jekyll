**The Lobby** (Abbr. **TL**) (August 2005 - November 2006) was a forum originally founded in August 2005 by  [AnubisBlessing](/wiki/index.php/User_AnubisBlessing/) and Raziel, although quite soon after that Loop joined the staff and the activity continued on the current forums after being moved from the original location.

### The story behind

Around the time of the creation of TL the bigger part of the LX community was based on [Gaming Universe](/wiki/index.php/Gaming_Universe/), where there was general discontent against the moderating team of the forums. The players felt neglected and disrespected by the mods and admins, which led to bad air between the two parts and culminated in bans of members which were not approved of by the community. So a small group of users emigrated to make their own forums and try to convince the community that it was ripe to move out of the hosted forums on GU.

#### Features

The idea of TL was to create a forum for players by players, and try to move out the activity from GU. The most popular section of GU, the clan threads were was remade and TL offered hosted board to clans or competition hosts not willing to make an own forum that would end up having too little activity. Basically everything GU had was improved upon and focused to the benefit of the player. However, the TL community would suffer from lack of development as the then most active modders, skinners and level makers would still be based on GU. Even if what were considered the most important clans joined the activity on the forums, it would seem hard to compete with the GU.
TL also implemented what was to be called the round table. Which was thought be be something like the alliance of LXA works now; a place for the the more established clans to exchange information and discuss events in the LX community. However it is questionable wether or not it worked as intended. But the biggest feature of all was the Filebase made by Loop and designed by Raziel.

### The death of TL

In times of an already split community - GU vs TL, [Classic](/wiki/index.php/Classic/) versus [Mortars](/wiki/index.php/Mortars/) - A new contender would appear on the forum arena. Made by thus far unknown players mainly from the mortar world it would end up consuming the activity on both GU and TL and prove to be the place where both mortar and classic players would coexist and eventually, the international forum scene would be dominated by the [Liero Xtreme Alliance](/wiki/index.php/Liero_Xtreme_Alliance/). In November 2006 TL was locked for public posting.
