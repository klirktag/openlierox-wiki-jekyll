**SimpleX Modding team** (active in 2004 - 2005) was a team formed by [HocusPocus](/wiki/index.php/HocusPocus/) in June 2004 to work on various LieroX Mods and packs.

## Projects

### LieroX-Mas

LieroX-Mas Mod is a Mod with 34 Christmas related weapons. SimpleX Modding team also made Christmas theme Levels, Skins and Frontend which were released in [Liero Xtreme v0.56 Christmas Edition](/wiki/index.php/Liero_Xtreme_v0.56_Christmas_Edition/).

### Unfinished

As a 2nd project SimpleX Modding team was planning to create LieroX Mod, a mod that would be a representative of the LX scripting capabilities. It was never finished or released. Same happened with their 3rd project - Anime Mod.
