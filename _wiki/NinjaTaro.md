**NinjaTaro** is one of the most popular and common used Skins. It was originally created by Mortis in 2 variations - NinjaTaro and NinjaTaroDark. NinjaTaroDarkB was the third modification of it. A while later [Raziel](/wiki/index.php/Raziel/) edited the original Skin into another popular version - Yodataroraz, which was made color friendly more than 3 years later by SorZ. Skin is still being edited often today and there exists a lot of modified versions which are not worth of mentioning because nobody is using them.

## Versions

### NinjaTaro

![](/wiki/images/thumb/0/06/NinjaTaro.PNG/100px-NinjaTaro.PNG)

**NinjaTaro** is a first edition of this ninja themed skin, made by Mortis. Although it is one of the most used Skins, some people blame it for a bit poor aiming animation - you can see where player is aiming only by movement of eyes, which is identical in few different frames of the skin.

### NinjaTaroDark (NinjaTaroDarkA)

![](/wiki/images/thumb/f/f4/NinjaTaroDark.PNG/100px-NinjaTaroDark.PNG)

**NinjaTaroDark** is a second version of NinjaTaro, made by Mortis. It differs from NinjaTaro in color of the face and eyes. NinjaTaroDarkA is just another name for this Skin. Sometime while creating a new LieroX pack somebody added "A" to the Skin's name and caused a duplication. Most of the LieroX players now have this Skin under both names.

### NinjaTaroDarkB

![](/wiki/images/thumb/c/c5/NinjaTaroDarkB.PNG/100px-NinjaTaroDarkB.PNG)

**NinjaTaroDarkB** is a third version of NinjaTaro which is practically identical to NinjaTaroDark, the only difference is in eyes - this one has red eyes. It was made by unknown artist as a slight edit of NinjaTaroDark. Some people claim it might belong to the original creator - Mortis, but this can not be confirmed.

### Yodataroraz

![](/wiki/images/thumb/4/45/Yodataroraz.PNG/100px-Yodataroraz.PNG)

**Yodataroraz** is a modification of NinjaTaro made by [Raziel](/wiki/index.php/Raziel/) in late 2004. Skin is associated with Yoda character from Star Wars. In January 2008 SorZ made modifications of Yodataroraz with Raziel's permission. He edited the skin to a color change friendly one and also made 2 variations as they can be found in original NinjaTaro Skin - YodaTaroDarkA, YodaTaroDarkB.

## Remakes

### NinjaTaro(Remade)

![](/wiki/images/thumb/b/ba/NinjaTaroRemade.png/100px-NinjaTaroRemade.png)

**NinjaTaro(Remade)** was created from scratch by [Asrack](/wiki/index.php/User_Asrack/) on April 30th to make a new smoother looking NinjaTaro. It is colour friendly, and works the same way as the original Ninja Taro - it is aiming with eyes. It differs from Mortis' NinjaTaro in smoothness which made it look more pixelated, and the hands and legs are more realistic.
