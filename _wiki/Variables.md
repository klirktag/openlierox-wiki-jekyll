All ingame settings can be accessed via variables.

To get a list, currently the easiest way is to use autocompletion in [ingame console](/wiki/index.php/Ingame_console/) on the "setVar" command or with [chat commands](/wiki/index.php/Chat_commands/) on the "/setVar" command. You can also take a look into [options.cfg](/wiki/index.php/Options.cfg/) to get a list.

Some common variables:

```
GameOptions.GameInfo.TimeLimit - the time limit in minutes
GameOptions.Network.ServerName - the servername
```
