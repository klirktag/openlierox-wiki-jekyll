**Magus** (active in August 2004 - September 2007) was a member of the LieroX community. He lives in Canada and is a friend of Azriath, Asrack, and Sirus.

Magus was a [rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) and [limited](/wiki/index.php/Limited/) player who started his career in Asrack's [Angels of Death](/wiki/index.php/Angels_of_Death/) clan. He made a few levels. Magus was wildly known in many clans and became a ruthless killing machine in clan wars. He racked up his clanwar record in the [LcD] clan embarassing other clans on Lamda Bunker with Asrack or just straight pwning with Cloud. He was also known for relentless flames towards players with stupid LieroX logic.

## AMA

AMA was a production team created by [Asrack](/wiki/index.php/User_Asrack/). It stood for Asrack, Magus, and Azriath. They created 3 levels, 1 frontend, and 1 skin. Asrack was a programmer and web designer, with Magus and Azriath making graphics. Making 2 websites, and 1 LX game that was never released which was an edited source code that fixed the size of skins, and some other slight things.

Magus created 3 levels in this time, AMA Dome being a duel level which was the AMA starting level. As with the AMA frontend, helped by Spectro. Got huge hits.
