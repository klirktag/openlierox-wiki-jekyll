[IRC](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_Relay_Chat) is a very old protocol for chatting in rooms (also called channels) together with a bunch of people.

There is one main IRC channel for [OpenLieroX](/wiki/index.php/OpenLieroX/): #LieroX on irc.freenode.org.

Since OpenLieroX 0.58, you are automatically connected with this channel if you start OLX. That is the chat you can see in the net menu.

All (O)LX interested people and players chat there about games or other unrelated stuff and sometimes the development team also meets there to chat.
