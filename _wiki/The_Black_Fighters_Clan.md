**The Black Fighters Clan** (**TbfC.ee |** ) (June 2004 - March 2005, February 2007 - April 2007) was one of the greatest combo clans in time of their existence. They were being recognised for the use of a red Aimman skin. Clan was originally playing only [Lamda Bunker](/wiki/index.php/Lamda_Bunker/) [Rifles](/wiki/index.php/Rifles/) setting but later they would also include [Jailbreak](/wiki/index.php/Jailbreak/), Jukke Dome and Liero Factory.

## Controversy

TbfC.ee was being often declared as a clan of cheaters for their use of the so-called Rope Trick. At some point it got proven that TbfC.ee members were using an edited version of [Lamda Bunker](/wiki/index.php/Lamda_Bunker/) level. It left a black mark on the clan and although being a very skilled clan TbfC.ee could never improve their reputation after that.
