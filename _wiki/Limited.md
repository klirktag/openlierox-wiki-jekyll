**Limited** setting is a non-combo setting, played with 100% [Loading Time](/wiki/index.php/Loading_Time/). The weapon sets can differ in servers depending on what weapons people like.

## History

Limited 100 % Loading Time setting most likely originates from [Firebird](/wiki/index.php/Firebird/) tournament. First pick of weapons was also including Bazooka and Explosives beside the common pick you can still find today. Use of Handgun or Chaingun was very rare as the better alternative for it was Bazooka.

## Weapons

### Common pick

* Cannon
* Chaingun or Handgun.
* Doomsday
* Napalm
* Super Shotgun

### Includes in uncommon pick

* Dart
* Blaster
* Explosives
* Flamer
* Grasshopper
* Minigun
* Minirockets
* Uzi
* Winchester
