# Welcome

In a wiki, it's everyones job to add everything they know. And since everyone can edit an article, so may you! Start by practicing with your own user article. An article about yourself.
On your right hand side, in the menu you should find a link with your name. Click it and start editing!

If you need help, there is a [general help article](/wiki/index.php/Help_Contents/) in which you can learn some guidelines.

Have fun, enjoy your stay, and happy reading!
