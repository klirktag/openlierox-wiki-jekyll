Laggers are people who suffer from high ping, because of reasons including: Geographical location and Internet connection. Most Laggers generally come from areas such as Australia or New Zealand, but players from all over the world are affected by it. A European player will lag in a North American server and vice-versa. However the term lagger may refer to anyone that a player does'nt like and can be used as a derogatory word against anyone.
