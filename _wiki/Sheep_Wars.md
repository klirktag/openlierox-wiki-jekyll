**Sheep Wars** (April 22, 2005 - May 23, 2005) was a tournament inspired by [Jukke](/wiki/index.php/Jukke/)'s The Sheep Story Comics and hosted by Avye. It was planed to have several phases as a The Sheep Story Comics adaptation but it ended after the first one because of lack of the interest and activity.

## Plot

### Phase 1: Primal Aggression (April 27, 2005)

The Sheep and Lieros first come out of existence; and the Sheep decide that living in the ground is NOT for them! The curious Lieros follow the Sheep up to the land above, and soon the Lieros become jealous of the Sheep! War breaks out between the two rival species.

### Phase 2: Enslavement + Rebellion (May 1, 2005)

After the overwhelming defeat of the Sheep, the worms have thrown the sheeps into slavery. Many have fallen. 500 years later, Sheeps are executed, used as cheap labor, and cannon fodder. Morale weakened, the sheep give in. Suddenly, just when things couldn't get worse, a band of Sheep have reported to have joined forces with a mysterious, "Evil Sheep Lord". 1-out lives will be subracted from those who have been out.

Choose an out person from each side. From the sheep side, He/She will be known as "Evil Sheep Lord". From the worm side, he/she will be known as "Worm Alpha". For the duration of the contest, he will be this. However, they only have 2 out-lives, and their old scores will be deleted.

## Rules

Players had to use Skins respectively to whichever army they have belonged to. Sheep army had to use a Sheep themed Skin, made by [Jukke](/wiki/index.php/Jukke/). Liero Worm army had to use the LieroX worm, made by [Strider](/wiki/index.php/Strider/). It was forbidden for contestants to use the Anti-Sheep Skin.

Players on the same team were gathering together to defeat the opposing team. Each team could choose whoever they wanted to go with to the war. Each players had "3-out" lives which meant player could be out of the game in total of 3 times per whole Sheep Wars. In other words if a player got out in one battle, the next game he played, he could only get out 2 more times.

Players were allowed to use any mod and loading time they liked. However, the level was being selected beforehand. There was no limit in how many players could play per one team. It could have been from 1 vs 7 to 4 vs 4. Duels between the contestants were allowed but limited to one per phase.

### Point system equation

```
[Lives ^ (9/10) + Kills] = Contestant's points
```
