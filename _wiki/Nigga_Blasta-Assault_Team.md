**Nigga Blasta - Assault Team - [NB-AT]**

## History

Gigolo, the founder of NB-AT, started NB-AT in year 2004. The clan was always medium-strong. It was not taken to the gang of "the best". Gigolo and his North American friends used to play a lot in those days. NB-AT first fell, when there was made clan ( V ), Gigolo moved there and left NB-AT for good. He brought it back in year 2007 first half, and took many good players with him to NB-AT. In 2008 beginning, Tafka and JePPe decided to move to NB-AT. Soon later Namura came too. Then NB-AT became and international clan. Now NB-AT can be considered to be one of the strongest clans.
