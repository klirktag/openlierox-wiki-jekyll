# Game Concepts

## Getting around

You can define your controls in the settings from the main menu of the game.
You move left and right and aim up or down with your respective keys. You can also get over obstacles by jumping, and even higher such by using the [Ninja rope](/wiki/index.php/Ninja_rope/). When you encounter obstacles that are of dirt, you may destroy them by  [digging](/wiki/index.php/Game_concepts/#digging) or blasting them with your weapons

### Firing and weapons

The weapons you can choose at a given moment is decided by the [mod](/wiki/index.php/Mod/) that has been loaded.
You fire your weapon with your fire button. The effects of a weapon can vary greatly. Read more in the main article: weapon

### Digging

You may dig though dirt by holding the key which moves you in the direction you want to go, and tapping "backwards". So if you want to dig to the right, you hold right and tap (left, left, left) on your keyboard. Digging with a joy pad is done by clicking repeatedly in the direction you want to dig.
The direction you aim when digging will affect the angle you will make your cave in.
