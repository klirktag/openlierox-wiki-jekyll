# The Palace Series

The palace like levels made by Legend between years 2007-2008. Levels usually lack original LieroXtreme level features, but on the other hand provide lots of unusual things. To get these levels either search from thelobby filebase or LXAlliance downloads.

## Palaces

* Desert Palace - Palace that started this serie. Background decision shares opinions, as the level overall does. Simple gameplay makes the level convenient to play with.

* Ancient Palace - Likewise Desert Palace, also Ancient Palace has loads of 90¬∞ angles. Level is pretty big, so gameplay is mostly fast and open.

* Water Palace - Gameplay is very open, like suitable for mortars. Contains rare humour from Legend, as the fishes' layot indicates.

* Ice Palace - The first really serious attempt, author's one of the favourites. Level is capable to handle big size battles.

* Shadow Palace - Doesn't really suit many players, as the game would be total chaos in this large size map. Optimal for tight 2vs2 battles.

* Great Palace - Big. Large. Massive. Huge. Are there more adjectives?

* Sacred Palace - Compared to previous levels there can be detected some development. Symmetric is the most predominating feature.

* Darkness Palace - Similar to Sacred Palace, though moving is a lot of more limited indoors. Completely dark background reminds a bit Ancient Palace. Completely symmetric again.
