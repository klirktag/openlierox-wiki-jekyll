This clan was started by Bart and QuiverS in the year of 2011.

Introduction
Due to inactivity of Bart and QuiverS former LX-partners, aka members/friends of WvW, AND due to lack of drama within LXA lately, QuiverS and Bart decided to head for new directions.
These new directions , more specifically, withhold the beginning of a new duo-clan, which will be named after two very remarkable players in LieroX: the name will be [bkS]. Up to you to find out who this is about! ;-)

They knew that it may have looked like they were letting down our former clanmembers, and in a way they were, I guess, but I think none of you shall invalidate the fact that WvW had been very inactive lately and consisted mainly just of QuiverZ and Bart, so they figured they might as well go without them.

Bart and QuiverS would as well like to thank all of these people, for giving them a great time and the experience to take on their own little project here. They do hope to remain good friends and to meet everyone on the battlefield.
