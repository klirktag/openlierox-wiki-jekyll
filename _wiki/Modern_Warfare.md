**Modern Warfare** is a [instagib](/wiki/index.php/Instagib/) mod. It was created by Scazzarus with help of other members at GU. It is popular for its flashy graphics, and over powered weapons. Most common setting picked is [Mortars](/wiki/index.php/Mortars/). Mortars is usually played by newcomers to the game. Though there are some long time LieroX players who play the setting on occation as well. Mostly Mortars is not considered a good setting among many long time LX players, which again lays out a conflict between new and old players.

Another setting is the limited version for this game. [MW Limited](/wiki/index.php/MW_Limited/) which is played in 100lt with many weapon bans.
