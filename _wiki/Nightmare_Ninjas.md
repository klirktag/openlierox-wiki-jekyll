**Nightmare Ninjas** (**[nn]**) (March 04 - Spring 2005) was a [NinjaTaro](/wiki/index.php/NinjaTaro/) skin only using clan open for all sorts of settings. Clan was often being flamed by Rifles only playing clans as well as Limited only playing clans for their lack of skills.

## History

Nightmare Ninjas were established in March 2004 by Shadow and Mads Kills-x. Clan was active until Mads Kills-x has left the community in 2005.

Shadow wanted to revive the clan in Summer of 2005. Clan was up for 2 months until inactivity put the final end to Nightmare Ninjas.
