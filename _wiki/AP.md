# AP

AP, which means Alexis Power, is a kind of alliance for [Xis](/wiki/index.php/Xis/) and [Calvi](/wiki/index.php/Calvi/), because they have got the same real name : Alexis. The two players who have "# AP" at the end of their nick belong to [RIP](/wiki/index.php/RIP/).

The legend says that, when AP's two powers are playing together, the Alexis's wisdom and power are at their best.
