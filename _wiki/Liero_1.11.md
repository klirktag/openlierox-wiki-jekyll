# Liero 1.11

Basically this is just a more balanced version of the original [Liero 1.0](/wiki/index.php/Liero_1.0/) or Classic. The mod uses weapon delays to make it less laggy when playing across the world servers. Recoil on some weapons has been removed to make them easier to use and damage has been removed.

## Settings

It's barely played for anything but [Unlimited](/wiki/index.php/Unlimited/).
