**42** (**[42]**) (July 2004 - November 2005) was one of the most important clans in LieroX history. 42 clan members were not very active in game but they contributed a lot to LieroX community outside the servers. They made several popular levels, mods and skins people are still playing and using today. Members of the 42 clan were local moderators for LieroX section in [Gaming Universe](/wiki/index.php/Gaming_Universe/) as well as OPs in LieroX IRC channel. They were helping [JasonB](/wiki/index.php/JasonB/) with the development of LieroX and also hosting their own LieroX portal with forums - [LieroX.net](/wiki/index.php/LieroX.net/) .

## History

Clan was born as a merger between [heh](/wiki/index.php/Heh/) and [erm](/wiki/index.php/Erm/) in July 2004.

Clan's activity stopped in September 2005 when members of the clan would start claiming they are waiting for new version of LieroX to be released. After the release of newer version activity in game has risen but only for a shorter period.

Although clan was never declared dead there was next to impossible to notice a 42 clan member in game after late Fall of 2005. KOH was the only member who would every now and then play the game until he finally gave up in September 2006.
